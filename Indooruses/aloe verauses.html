Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is a succulent plant species of the genus Aloe. It is well-known for its thick, fleshy leaves that contain a gel-like substance used in various health and beauty products.

Uses of Aloe Vera:

  • Skin hydration and healing of burns.
  • Soothing effect on sunburns.
  • Used in cosmetics for its moisturizing properties.
  • May aid in digestion when consumed.
